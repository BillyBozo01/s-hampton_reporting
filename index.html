Tobacco Law – Key Points (UK)

  • Age restriction: no sales to under 18s.
  • Health warnings required; no single cigarettes.
  • Illicit/counterfeit tobacco sales are illegal.
  • Advertising and promotion are heavily restricted.

This is a simplified summary, not legal advice.

Vapes & E‑cigarettes – Key Points (UK)

  • No sales to under 18s.
  • Limits on nicotine strength and tank size for nicotine products.
  • Packaging and health warnings required; some ads restricted.
  • Non‑compliant or counterfeit devices/liquids must not be sold.

Always check the latest local Trading Standards guidance.
