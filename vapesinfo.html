Vapes & E-cigarettes – Key Points (UK)

Rules on selling vape products.

Report Tobacco Law Info Vape Law Info Privacy
  • No sales to under 18s.
  • Too much nicotine. Any vapes with a nictoine content over 20mg/ml is illegal
  • Maximum tank capacity 2ml
  • Liquid must not exceed 10ml
  • Packaging must be child-resistant and tamper-evident
  • Health warnings and ingridients and batch references must be present of packing
  • Non-compliant or counterfeit devices/liquids must not be sold.
  • It is illegal to stock, sell or supply disposable vapes
  • Vapes are considered disposible if:
  • The battery cannot be recharged
  • The fuild cannot be refileld

Always check the latest local Trading Standards guidance.
