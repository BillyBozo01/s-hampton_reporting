Vapes & E-cigarettes – Key Points (UK)

Rules on selling vape products.

  • Max nicotine strength and tank size apply.
  • Age restriction: no sales to under 18s.
  • Product labelling and safety requirements apply.
  • Vapes are considered disposable if:
  • The battery cannot be recharged
  • The fluid cannot be refilled
