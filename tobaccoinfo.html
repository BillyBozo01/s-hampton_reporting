Tobacco Law – Key Points (UK)

Important rules on selling tobacco products.

Report Tobacco Law Info Vape Law Info Privacy
  • Age restriction: no sales to under 18s.
  • Health warnings required; no single cigarettes.
  • Illicit/counterfeit tobacco sales are illegal.
  • Advertising and promotion are heavily restricted.

This is a simplified summary, not legal advice.
